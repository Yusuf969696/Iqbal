General Insurance

For more than 20 years we have provided comprehensive insurance services to thousands of clients and our staff are very experienced in the placement of corporate and personal risks.

We have insurance facilities with both Irish & overseas insurers and we offer skilled professional and impartial advice on all classes of Commercial & Personal Lines Insurance.

Our unique approach means that our Clients have one port of call for all their Insurance, Protection and Investment needs. We value our client engagements and would welcome any enquiries which you have.

Helping you achieve your financial goals

Welcome to PM Brennan Financial Brokers, we are a composite firm of financial advisors serving our clients for more than 40 years and we pride ourselves on being able to offer superior service, impartial advice and a wide range of investment & insurance solutions. For decades we have successfully worked with clients ranging from large corporates to individuals to help in protecting & growing their assets.

We are proud of our heritage and are confident we can provide you with impartial advice, expert analysis and an efficient & professional advisory service. Relationships with our clients are based on mutual trust, are long term in nature and we aim to be your advisor of choice when it comes to financial planning, wealth management, general insurance & health insurance advisory services.
